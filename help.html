Frequently Asked Questions

Below you will find FAQs that were most commonly get about the Activity Tracker. If your question is not listed below, then please send us an E-mail (support@activitytracker.com).

Feature FAQs

How do I find my information?

All of your information can be found on the Dashboard.

How do I input data?

Go to the Add Data page. You can submit your data there.

Is the weather accurate?

Yes! All of our data is sourced from the OpenWeatherMap API.

Can I get a free trial?

No need! Our Activity Tracker is completely free as we are fully supported by ADs.

How does the weather page find related images?

All of the images used are found through the Unsplash random image API.

How do I access my achievements?

This feature hasn't been released yet. We hope to add this feature soon.

Can I link my Strava account?

Not just yet. This feature is being worked on and will be released soon.

How could I submit a question that isn't here?

You can send us an email on support@activitytracker.com.

Support FAQs

What athletes use your Tracker?

Nobody currently, however we are open to applications.

Does the tracker get updated?

Yes! The Activity Tracker is supported by an active team of developers.

Do you have licenses to all of the images?

All the images used fall under the Creative Commons License. If there is a problem please contact us.

Creator FAQs

Who created the Activity Tracker?

The tracker was created by a small team of students at Manchester Metropolitan University.

When was the tracker made?

The Activity Tracker was initially created in March 2022, but we are still constantly improving it every day!
